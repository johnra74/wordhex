About WordHéx

This game was inspired by the original Wordle. One word a day wasn't enough for me. I wanted another round without waiting 24-hours. I'm sure plenty feel the same. I decided to create this and expand it to 6-letter words. What can I say other than, "Imitiation is the sincerest form of flattery." Enjoy!

Guess the 6-letter (Héx) word of the day in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 6-letter (Héx) word. Hit the to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
